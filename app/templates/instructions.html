How to Play

  • The game board consists of 9 sub-boards (3x3), forming a larger 3x3 grid.
  • You are Player X, and the AI is Player O.
  • When you click on a cell in a sub-board, you send your opponent to that corresponding sub-board.
  • If the sub-board is already won or full, your opponent can move anywhere.
  • Win a sub-board like regular tic-tac-toe (three in a row).
  • Win the meta board by winning 3 sub-boards in a row.
